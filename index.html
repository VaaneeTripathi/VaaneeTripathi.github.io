Vaanee Tripathi

About

I'm a Computer Science student at Ashoka University, passionate about creating accessible and inclusive technology that makes a meaningful impact in people's lives.

My work focuses on accessibility research and development, where I design solutions for visually impaired learners and develop tools that make technology more inclusive. From creating tactile learning kits to building refreshable dot grids, I believe technology should be accessible to everyone.

As President of the Women in Computing Society, I organized India's first student-led accessibility tech conference and lead initiatives that foster an inclusive CS community. I'm also a Makerspace mentor, helping students bring their innovative ideas to life through hands-on learning and prototyping.

Works&Projects

Academic

Accessible Intro to CS Curriculum

Comprehensive 5-pronged strategy to improve accessibility in CS education, featuring tactile learning kits and inclusive curriculum design validated through user testing with visually impaired students.

Refreshable Tactile Dot Grid

Accepted at ATIA 2025 Conference and HCII International 2025. A frugal device using OpenCV to teach visual concepts in mathematics and computer science to visually impaired learners.

Web Accessibility Enhancer Tool

Browser extension leveraging axe-core and generative AI to make non-screen reader-friendly websites accessible in real-time, improving web accessibility for users with disabilities.

Non-academic

The Air Purifier Project

Open-source, frugal air purification device designed to make clean air accessible. Features sensor-based validation system and focuses on low-cost materials for widespread adoption.

Food Health Monitor

IIT Bombay internship project developing innovative solutions for food spoilage detection and health monitoring. Provisional patent pending for this early warning system.

GenArt Performance Technology

Novel artistic medium combining projections, performance, and technology. Led hardware research and creative development at the intersection of art and technology.
